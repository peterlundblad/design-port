S.F. Poster

Creative Brief: This project is an exploration of implementing real-world textures into illustration.

Role: Visual Designer

Process: I chose to focus on the tech sphere and built my lettering out of html. Those who are rather curious will find that the code itself points to more specific references to San Francisco’s history. To signal the underlying message, I highlighted a few keywords in bright contrasting colors.
