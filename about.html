About Peter

Peter is a senior visual designer, specializing in brand communications and illustration. He is passionate about creating visual content that is effective, dynamic, and absolutely beautiful.

Previously, he was a Senior Designer on the product marketing team of CBR, a biotech company in the family health space.

Additionally, Peter is a photographer, baker, and part-time punslinger. Like all designers, he LOVES coffee and La Croix (shout out to the Tangerine and Mango peeps).
