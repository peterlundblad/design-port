About Peter

Peter is a freelance art director and designer with a focus on brand, type, and motion design. He’s passionate about crafting visual content that’s not only effective and dynamic but also beautifully crafted and memorable.

With over a decade of experience, Peter has with major brands, including Banana Republic, Pottery Barn, Warner Brothers, and Cin7. He has also managed creative teams and overseen multiple successful campaigns, bringing a balance of strategic vision and creative expertise to each project.

Beyond his design work, Peter is also an accomplished calligrapher, baker, and part-time punslinger. Like many designers, he has a well-documented love for coffee and La Croix.
