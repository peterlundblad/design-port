About Peter

Peter is a senior multidisciplinary designer, specializing in brand and illustration. He is passionate about creating visual content that is effective, dynamic, and absolutely beautiful.

Previously, he is was Senior Designer on the product marketing team of CBR, a biotech company in the family health space.

Besides design, Peter is a photographer, baker, and part-time punslinger. Like all designers, he LOVES coffee.
